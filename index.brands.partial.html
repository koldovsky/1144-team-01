OUR BRANDS

We sell tobacco products under several subsidiary brands. Look for these brands in stores in your city.

  • FINE CIGAR

    Imported from Cuba, these machine-made cigars use select natural leaf wrappers and aged
    filler tobaccos to emphasize their nice flavor and aroma. Produced since 2000, this brand
    included in the Havana family has a huge following among American smokers.

  • CIGAR WORLD

    Handmade in Cuba, the Cigar World cigars display the finest aged binder and filler tobaccos
    surrounded by natural wrapper for great flavor with that special Havana touch. The rich
    tobacco sweetness is accentuated with hearty notes of dark roast coffee.

  • CIGAR BAR

    Cigar Bar cigarillos are hand-rolled in Cuba with natural leaf wrappers. Choose the Aroma
    version dipped in cognac, the Sweet Filtered cognac-dipped or the strong Rum version. Allow
    yourself an evening with good tobacco.
